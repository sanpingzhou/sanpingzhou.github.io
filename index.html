I am a Ph.D candidate at Institute of Artificial Intelligence and Robotics at Xian Jiaotong University (XIJU). I am fortunately advised by Prof. Jinjun Wang. who is a very active international scholar in the field of Multimedia Computing and Pattern Recognition. From October 1 , 2018, I work in the Human Sensing Lab at Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) as a visiting researcher. My supervisor is Dr. Dong Huang. during my one year's study at CMU. My research interest includes computer vision and machine learning. Currently, I am most interested in the deep learning based person re-identification, saliency detection, multi-target tracking, self-paced learning and medical image segmentation algorithms.
